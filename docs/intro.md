# Welcome to SowTempo

SowTempo is a privacy-focused vegetable garden planning and management application designed for thoughtful gardeners who want to grow more food with less guesswork.

![SowTempo Dashboard](/img/screenshots/dashboard-overview.png)

## What is SowTempo?

SowTempo brings together everything you need for successful vegetable gardening:

- **Visual Garden Designer** — Design your garden beds with an intuitive canvas, place plants with proper spacing, and see your garden come to life
- **Smart Planting Schedule** — Get personalized planting dates based on your location and frost dates, with a full-year calendar view
- **Comprehensive Plant Database** — Browse nearly 4,000 vegetable varieties with detailed growing information
- **Growing Degree Days (GDD) Tracking** — Predict plant maturity more accurately using heat accumulation instead of just counting days
- **Harvest & Yield Tracking** — Record your harvests and analyze your garden's productivity over time
- **Complete Record Keeping** — Journal entries, pest encounters, soil tests, and watering logs all in one place

## Privacy First

Unlike other garden apps, SowTempo keeps your data where it belongs — on your device:

- **No account required** — Start planning immediately without signing up
- **No data collection** — We don't track you or harvest your data
- **No cloud dependency** — Your garden plans work offline, always
- **Open weather data** — Weather powered by Open-Meteo, free and privacy-respecting

## Who is SowTempo For?

SowTempo is designed for:

- **Home gardeners** who want to plan and track their vegetable gardens systematically
- **Beginner gardeners** who need guidance on when to plant based on their local climate
- **Experienced growers** who want detailed record-keeping and analytics
- **Privacy-conscious users** who don't want their gardening data in the cloud
